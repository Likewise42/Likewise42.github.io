Projects

Paychex Flex

Paychex Flex is a web app that allows small businesses to manage payroll and other managerial tasks with ease. I worked on the effort to make the dashboard customizable, as well as work to allow external applications to connect to Flex, and the migration off of Flash and onto Flex in late 2020. Worked with Angular, Spring Boot, and .Net.

The Caller Queue

The Caller Queue is a system for live shows to stage viewers before going on air. I worked on the third (and most recent) version which utilized Node.js, React, Redux, Socket.io, WebRTC, and MySQL. My work on the Caller Queue has been used by shows such as MLB's Chatting Cage and Daily Blast Live.
